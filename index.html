Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions customers ask about Interior works.

How long will it take to complete Interior work? +
Normally 20 to 45 days depending on design and work type. Modular kitchen may take 10-20 days.
Do you provide warranty for Interior works? +
Yes. We provide warranty depending on materials and service type. Contact us for details.
Do you provide free site visit and consultation? +
Yes. Free consultation and site visit is available in and around BHEL / Hyderabad.
Can I customize my design? +
Yes. All our designs are fully customizable based on your space and requirements.
Do you work outside BHEL area? +
Yes. We serve all areas across Hyderabad, including Gachibowli, Kondapur, Kukatpally, Miyapur, Jubilee Hills, and other locations.
What is the minimum budget required for home interior? +
Our Basic Package starts from approximately ₹1.5 Lakhs for essential interiors. We offer flexible packages to suit all budgets — contact us for a free custom quote.
